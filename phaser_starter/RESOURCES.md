# Game Development Resources

This document contains useful resources for game development, tools, assets, and services that can help with the RPG project.

## Audio & Voice

### ElevenLabs.io
[ElevenLabs.io](https://elevenlabs.io) is an AI-powered text-to-speech and voice cloning platform that provides high-quality, natural-sounding voice synthesis. It's particularly useful for generating NPC dialogue, narration, and voiceovers for games. The platform offers various voice models, supports multiple languages, and allows for fine-tuning of voice characteristics like tone, speed, and emotion. For RPG development, it can be invaluable for creating unique voices for different characters without needing voice actors, making it easier to add immersive dialogue and storytelling elements to the game.

## Sound Effects

### Mobile Apps

#### AI Sound Effect Generator (Mobile App)
AI Sound Effect Generator is a mobile app that lets you type a description and turns it into a short custom sound effect, with a simple interface aimed at quick SFX creation.

#### AI Sound Effects Generator (Android)
AI Sound Effects Generator is an Android app that generates "studio-quality" effects from text prompts, with options to preview, save, and export sounds for videos, games, and podcasts.

### Web-Based Generators

#### ElevenLabs Sound Effects
[ElevenLabs Sound Effects](https://elevenlabs.io) is a browser tool where you describe any sound and get multiple high-quality SFX variations you can download, including a free tier.

#### Canva AI Sound Effect Generator
Canva AI Sound Effect Generator is built into Canva via the Aurora Sound FX app, so you can prompt sounds and drop them directly into video or social content projects.

#### Adobe Firefly Sound Effects Generator
[Adobe Firefly Sound Effects Generator](https://firefly.adobe.com) is a web-based tool where you describe or perform the sound, then adjust and layer effects for use in podcasts, films, games, or social clips.

#### MyEdit AI Sound Effect Generator
MyEdit AI Sound Effect Generator is an online text-to-sound tool that creates custom SFX from prompts with quick preview and download, including a free option.

#### OptimizerAI
[OptimizerAI](https://optimizerai.com) is a web service focused on AI sound FX for creators and game developers, positioned as a fast way to "just generate" the effect you need.

#### MusicGPT
MusicGPT can create sound effects, samples, loops, and soundscapes from prompts, not just music, aiming at realistic audio for creative projects.

## Graphics & Art Assets

### PixelLab.ai
[PixelLab.ai](https://www.pixellab.ai) is an AI-powered platform designed to assist indie game developers in creating pixel art assets efficiently. It offers features such as one-click animations, skeleton-based animation, text-based animation, rotation tools for 4 and 8 directional views, style-consistent generation, true inpainting for editing, and environment tools for scene and map generation. The platform operates on cloud GPUs, eliminating the need for high-performance local hardware, and is compatible with Windows, Mac, and Linux. PixelLab also provides an API that enables developers to generate characters, animations, and environments dynamically, supporting procedural generation and live asset creation. For RPG development, it's an excellent tool for quickly generating pixel art sprites, character animations, and game environments while maintaining visual consistency.
